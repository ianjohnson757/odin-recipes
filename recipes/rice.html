The Disappearing Rice

Consider it Gone!!!

There is nothing better than well made bowl of fried rice. If you follow this recipe step by step, I can promise that you wont be disappointed.

  • Day Old Jasmine Rice
  • Peppers and Onion
  • 2 Eggs
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onion (garnish)

Time to get cooking:

  1. Preheat Skillet. Turn your skillet to medium high heat and pour sesame oil into the bottom of your skillet and add the onion, peppers, etc. Fry this until they are tender.
  2. Preheat a separate skillet add a little butter and pour in the rice. Cook until slightly browned.
  3. Slide the veggies to the side. Pour the beaten eggs onto the other side. Use a spatula to scramble the eggs. Mix them together with the veggies
  4. Add the rice. Combine it with the veggie and egg mixture. Pour the soy sauce on top and stir until it is heated throughout. Garnish with green onions.
